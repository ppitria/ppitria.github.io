About Me

Hi, I’m Pitriani 👋 I am a graduate of Informatics Engineering from UIN Sunan Gunung Djati Bandung. I am interested in data processing, automation, and building structured workflows that help make work more efficient.

My interest in this field started from academic projects that involved data analysis and data management. From there, I began exploring how data can be organized and processed into systems that support real tasks and improve efficiency.

I am comfortable working with Python, SQL, and Microsoft Excel for data processing, validation, and simple automation. I also have experience organizing project workflows, preparing documentation, and designing simple database simulations.

Through this portfolio, I document various projects — from data analysis to workflow-based tools and templates — as part of my continuous learning and development in building practical, structured solutions.
